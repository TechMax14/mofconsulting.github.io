Mock Interviews

Are you an entry-level engineer looking to land your dream job? Do you want to improve your coding skills and nail that technical interview? Look no further than our CodePath™ technical mock coding interview service. Our experienced team of engineers will put you through a rigorous mock interview, giving you the opportunity to practice your coding skills and receive valuable feedback. With our service, you'll gain the confidence and experience necessary to impress potential employers and secure your dream job.

Land your dream job!

  1. Who is this service for?

    • This service is ideal for entry-level software engineers who are looking to prepare for technical coding interviews for job applications.
    • It is also suitable for recent computer science graduates or individuals who are transitioning into a career in software development.

  2. What are the benefits?

    • Get personalized feedback on your performance in technical coding interviews.
    • Improve your problem-solving skills and algorithmic thinking.
    • Increase your confidence in job interviews and increase your chances of landing your dream job.
    • Practice in a safe and supportive environment to learn from your mistakes.
    • Access a range of technical coding interview questions that cover different levels of difficulty and topics.

  3. How does it work?

    • Schedule a mock technical coding interview with us.
    • Complete the technical coding interview, which will be conducted in a simulated real job interview environment.
    • Receive personalized feedback on your performance and areas for improvement.
    • Practice with a range of technical coding interview questions to improve your skills and build confidence.

  4. Why choose us?

    • Our team has years of experience in conducting technical coding interviews and providing personalized feedback to entry-level software engineers.
    • We use industry-standard technical coding interview questions to simulate the experience of a real job interview.
    • We offer a safe and supportive environment for engineers to practice and learn from their mistakes.
    • We provide a range of technical coding interview questions that cover different levels of difficulty and topics.
    • We are committed to helping you improve your skills and achieve your career goals.

At TMG we aim to listen to your goals, strengths, preferred roles/salary ranges to tailor customized and purpose-driven interviews to land your dream job. Don't let a lack of preparation hold you back - schedule now for our technical mock coding interview service today!

Resume Boosting

Review and Feedback

  • Our expert team of recruiters and software engineers will review your resume and provide feedback to help you make improvements. We'll assess your resume's format, content, and overall presentation to make sure it effectively showcases your skills and experience. Our feedback will help you highlight your strengths and stand out from other job applicants.

    • Optimization and Keyword Analysis

  • Our team will optimize your resume to ensure that it is optimized for applicant tracking systems (ATS) used by many companies to screen resumes. We will analyze your resume for keywords and phrases that are relevant to the software engineering industry, helping to increase your chances of being selected for an interview.

    • Professional Resume Writing

  • Our professional resume writers will work with you to create a customized resume that highlights your skills and experience in the best possible light. We'll collaborate with you to identify your strengths and accomplishments, and craft a resume that effectively showcases your achievements and expertise. With our professional resume writing services, you can feel confident that your resume will impress potential employers and increase your chances of landing your dream job.

    • Career Mentorship

    Mapping and Planning

  • Our experienced software engineering mentors will work with you to map out your career goals and develop a plan to achieve them. We'll assess your skills, interests, and experience, and help you identify potential career paths within the software engineering industry. Our mentors will provide guidance and support as you work towards your goals, and help you navigate any challenges along the way.

    • Soft Skill Development and Networking

  • In addition to technical expertise, soft skills such as communication, teamwork, and leadership are critical to success in the software engineering industry. Our mentors will provide guidance and coaching to help you develop these important skills. We'll also help you build your professional network, providing opportunities to connect with other software engineers and industry professionals.

    • Job Search Strategy

  • Job Search Strategy and Interview Preparation - Our mentors will provide guidance and support as you navigate the job search process. We'll also help you prepare for interviews, providing guidance on how to answer common interview questions and helping you build your confidence and interviewing skills.
